## Overview

### What is the Financials Module?

The Financials Module is your complete **financial management system** for real estate operations. It tracks every rupee in and out of your business through 9 specialized sub-modules.

**Think of it like this**: If your business is a ship, Financials is the navigation system showing where your money is coming from, where it's going, and where you'll end up.

1. **Check transaction status**: Must be "Completed"
2. **Check commission rate**: Must be set in deal
3. **Check property link**: Deal must link to property
4. **Manual override**: Edit and enter manually

#### Issue: Budget Variance Too High

**Problem**: Spending way over/under budget.

**Solutions**:
1. **Review budget**: Was it realistic?
2. **Check categorization**: Expenses in right buckets?
3. **Adjust budget**: If circumstances changed
4. **Tighten controls**: If overspending is issue

#### Issue: Cash Flow Problem

**Problem**: Running low on cash despite profitability.

**Solutions**:
1. **Review receivables**: Follow up on pending payments
2. **Review payables**: Delay non-urgent payments
3. **Review forecast**: Plan for shortage
4. **Arrange financing**: Short-term if needed

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Who can see financial data?**  
A: 
- **Agency Owner**: All data
- **Finance Manager**: All data
- **Agents**: Own commission only
- **Investors**: Own investments only

**Q: How often should I reconcile?**  
A: Monthly minimum, weekly recommended for active agencies.

**Q: Can I export financial data?**  
A: Yes! Export to Excel, PDF, or accounting software formats.

### Commission Questions

**Q: When is commission considered "earned"?**  
A: When the transaction is completed (Stage 7 in cycle).

**Q: How is tax calculated on commission?**  
A: Pakistan withholding tax (typically 12% for services).

**Q: What if a deal falls through after payment?**  
A: Commission is reversed. If already paid to agent, deducted from next payment.

### Budget Questions

**Q: Can I have multiple budgets?**  
A: Yes! Annual, quarterly, monthly, project-specific.

**Q: What if I go over budget?**  
A: Alert is shown. Review and adjust spending or revise budget.

**Q: Can budgets be revised mid-period?**  
A: Yes, but document the reason and get approval.
