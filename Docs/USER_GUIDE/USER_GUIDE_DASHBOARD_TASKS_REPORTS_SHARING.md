## Permission Levels

### View Only

**What they can do**:
- See all details
- View photos/documents
- Read notes and history

**What they can't do**:
- Edit anything
- Delete
- Share with others

**Use case**: Showing property to another agent's client

---

### Can Edit

**What they can do**:
- Everything in "View Only"
- Update property details
- Add notes
- Upload documents
- Log activities

**What they can't do**:
- Delete property
- Share with others
- Change ownership

**Use case**: Co-listing a property

---

### Full Access

**What they can do**:
- Everything in "Can Edit"
- Delete property
- Share with others
- Change settings
- Transfer ownership

**What they can't do**:
- (Nothing - they have full control)

**Use case**: Joint ownership or partnership

## Best Practices

### When to Share

âœ… **DO share when**:
- Co-listing property with another agent
- Referring client to specialist
- Collaborating on large deal
- Team needs visibility
- Training new agent

âŒ **DON'T share**:
- Without permission from client
- Confidential information
- With competitors (without reason)
- Permanently (use expiry dates)

### Security Tips

âœ… **DO**:
- Use lowest permission needed
- Set expiry dates
- Review shared items monthly
- Revoke when no longer needed
- Track who accesses what

âŒ **DON'T**:
- Give full access by default
- Share passwords/credentials
- Share client financial data
- Ignore access requests
- Forget to revoke old shares
