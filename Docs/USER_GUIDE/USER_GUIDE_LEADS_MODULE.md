## The 5-Stage Pipeline

### Overview

```
NEW â†’ CONTACTED â†’ QUALIFIED â†’ NEGOTIATION â†’ CLOSED
(Inquiry)  (Engaged)   (Serious)   (Discussing)  (Deal!)
```

### Stage 1: NEW
**What it means**: Fresh lead, not yet contacted.

**Lead state**:
- Just entered system
- No contact made yet
- Urgency: HIGH (contact within 24 hours!)

**Your actions**:
- Review lead details
- Research their requirements
- Prepare talking points
- Make first contact ASAP

**How long leads stay**: 0-24 hours (ideal)

**KPI**: First contact time < 4 hours

---

### Stage 2: CONTACTED
**What it means**: Initial contact made, gathering information.

**Lead state**:
- You've spoken/emailed
- Basic information collected
- Interest level being assessed

**Your actions**:
- Understand requirements deeply
- Ask qualifying questions
- Send relevant property listings
- Schedule viewings
- Add to follow-up schedule

**Qualifying Questions**:
```
1. Budget range?
2. Timeline to buy/sell?
3. Financing arranged or needed?
4. Decision makers involved?
5. Current situation (renting, owning)?
6. Must-have features?
7. Preferred locations?
8. Motivation level (1-10)?
```

**How long leads stay**: 1-7 days

**Move to next stage when**: Lead qualifies (serious buyer/seller)

---

### Stage 3: QUALIFIED
**What it means**: Serious lead with clear intent and ability.

**Lead qualifies when**:
- âœ“ Budget confirmed
- âœ“ Timeline within 3-6 months
- âœ“ Financing ready or pre-approved
- âœ“ Decision maker engaged
- âœ“ Clear requirements defined
- âœ“ Motivation score 7+

**Your actions**:
- Show relevant properties
- Provide market analysis
- Build relationship
- Address concerns
- Schedule multiple viewings
- Move toward offer

**How long leads stay**: 7-30 days

**Move to next stage when**: Lead ready to make offer or list

---

### Stage 4: NEGOTIATION
**What it means**: Active discussions on specific property/listing.

**Lead state**:
- Serious about specific property
- Discussing price/terms
- Preparing offer or listing

**Your actions**:
- Negotiate price
- Discuss terms
- Prepare agreements
- Address final concerns
- Close the deal

**How long leads stay**: 3-14 days

**Move to next stage when**: Agreement reached or lead lost

---

### Stage 5: CLOSED
**What it means**: Lead converted to deal or marked as lost/inactive.

**Two outcomes**:

**Won** âœ“:
```
Lead â†’ Deal Created â†’ Transaction Started
Commission opportunity secured!
```

**Lost** âœ—:
```
Lead â†’ Marked as Lost
Reason: [Budget, Timeline, Went elsewhere, etc.]
```

**Your actions (Won)**:
- Create Deal record
- Start transaction cycle
- Celebrate the win!

**Your actions (Lost)**:
- Document loss reason
- Add to nurture list (re-engage later)
- Learn from loss

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Issue: Lead Not Responding

**Problem**: Called/emailed multiple times, no response.

**Solutions**:
1. **Try different channels**:
   - Called? Try WhatsApp
   - Emailed? Try SMS
   - Morning calls? Try evening

2. **Change your approach**:
   - Instead of "Following up..."
   - Try "New listing you might like..."
   - Add value, don't just chase

3. **Set it aside**:
   - After 21 days, move to nurture
   - Monthly touches only
   - Don't waste time

#### Issue: Lead Stuck in Stage

**Problem**: Lead in CONTACTED for 30+ days.

**Solutions**:
1. **Qualify or Disqualify**:
   - Ask direct questions
   - "Are you still interested?"
   - "What's holding you back?"

2. **Identify blocker**:
   - Budget issues?
   - Timeline changed?
   - Found another property?

3. **Make a decision**:
   - Move to QUALIFIED if ready
   - Move to CLOSED (Lost) if not
   - Don't let leads rot

#### Issue: Too Many Leads

**Problem**: Can't keep up with follow-ups.

**Solutions**:
1. **Prioritize by score**:
   - Focus on HOT (80+) first
   - Then WARM (50-79)
   - Automate COLD (<50)

2. **Use templates**:
   - Email templates
   - WhatsApp templates
   - Save time

3. **Get help**:
   - Delegate to team
   - Hire assistant
   - Use automation

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: What's the difference between a lead and a deal?**  
A: A **lead** is a potential customer (interested but not committed). A **deal** is an active transaction (offer made, negotiating). Leads become deals.

**Q: How many leads should I have?**  
A: **Industry average**: 20-30 active leads per agent. Focus on quality over quantity.

**Q: What's a good conversion rate?**  
A: **Industry average**: 2-5% of leads become deals. Top performers: 10-15%.

**Q: How long should I follow up with a lead?**  
A: **Active follow-up**: 21 days. **Nurture**: 6-12 months. If no response after 21 days, move to low-priority nurture.

### Lead Scoring

**Q: Why is my lead score low?**  
A: Check the breakdown:
- Missing budget info?
- Vague timeline?
- No engagement (viewings)?
- Not qualified?

Update missing info to increase score.

**Q: Can I manually change a lead's score?**  
A: No, but you can update the data that affects score (budget, timeline, etc.).

**Q: Do lead scores update automatically?**  
A: Yes! As you log activities and update info, scores recalculate.

### 21-Day System

**Q: What if I contact them before Day 4?**  
A: Great! Mark the follow-up as complete. The system is a minimum, not maximum.

**Q: What if they don't respond by Day 21?**  
A: Move to nurture list (monthly touches). Don't chase unresponsive leads daily.

**Q: Can I customize the 21-day schedule?**  
A: Yes, use "Custom Follow-up" to create your own timing.

### Converting Leads

**Q: When should I convert a lead to a deal?**  
A: When they:
- Make an offer on a property
- Ask you to list their property
- Sign an agreement
- Commit to moving forward

**Q: What happens to the lead after conversion?**  
A: Lead marked as "Won" and moved to CLOSED stage. All history preserved. Deal created.

**Q: Can I convert a lead back if deal falls through?**  
A: Yes! Re-activate the lead and continue follow-up.
