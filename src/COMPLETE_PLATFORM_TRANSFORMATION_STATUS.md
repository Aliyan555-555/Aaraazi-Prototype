## 🎯 **UX LAWS APPLIED THROUGHOUT**

### **1. Fitts's Law (Targeting)**
✅ Large primary action buttons (44x44px minimum)  
✅ Optimal placement (top-right for primary actions)  
✅ Easy-to-click targets throughout  

### **2. Miller's Law (Cognitive Load)**
✅ Max 5 stats in headers and cards  
✅ Max 7 quick filters in search bars  
✅ Grouped related information  
✅ Clear information hierarchy  

### **3. Hick's Law (Decision Time)**
✅ Progressive disclosure (secondary actions in dropdowns)  
✅ Limited primary choices (1-3 max per context)  
✅ Advanced filters behind toggles  

### **4. Jakob's Law (Familiarity)**
✅ Standard patterns (search top-left, actions top-right)  
✅ Breadcrumbs in expected locations  
✅ Familiar table/grid patterns  

### **5. Aesthetic-Usability Effect**
✅ Professional, cohesive design throughout  
✅ Consistent spacing (8px grid system)  
✅ Smooth transitions and interactions  
✅ Enterprise-grade appearance
