# Requirements Navigation - Quick Reference Guide

## How to Access Requirement Details

### Buyer Requirements

1. **Navigate to Buyer Requirements Workspace**:
   - Click "Buyer Requirements" in sidebar under "Sales & Marketing"

2. **View Details**:
   - Click on any requirement card (anywhere on the card)
   - OR click the card in table view
   - Details page will open

3. **Actions from Details Page**:
   - **Back**: Returns to workspace
   - **Edit**: Edit requirement details
   - **Find Matches**: View matched properties
   - **Send Offer**: Send offer to buyer for a property
   - **View Property**: Click matched property → Navigate to property details
   - **View Sell Cycle**: Click offer → Navigate to sell cycle details

---

### Rent Requirements

1. **Navigate to Rent Requirements Workspace**:
   - Click "Rent Requirements" in sidebar under "Rentals"

2. **View Details**:
   - Click on any requirement card (anywhere on the card)
   - OR click "View Details" button in the card actions
   - Details page will open

3. **Actions from Details Page**:
   - **Back**: Returns to workspace
   - **Edit**: Edit requirement details
   - **Find Matches**: View matched properties
   - **View Property**: Click matched property → Navigate to property details
   - **View Rent Cycle**: Click on a cycle → Navigate to rent cycle details

---

## Quick Troubleshooting

### Issue: "Nothing happens when I click"
- **Check**: Make sure you're clicking ON the card, not outside it
- **Try**: Refresh the page and try again
- **Verify**: Check browser console for errors (F12)

### Issue: "Details page is blank"
- **Check**: The requirement ID is valid
- **Try**: Go back to workspace and click again
- **Verify**: Requirement still exists (hasn't been deleted)

### Issue: "Back button doesn't work"
- **Solution**: The back button always works - it's in the top-left of the details page
- **Alternative**: Use browser back button
- **Alternative**: Click "Buyer Requirements" or "Rent Requirements" in sidebar
